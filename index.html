What is Posture?

Posture is the way someone holds themself. Posture can show you how confident someone may be at a certain moment, or how they feel at a certain moment. Posture is critical in the way people present themselves. There are two types of postures: static posture and dynamic posture. Static posture is how someone holds themself when they aren't moving. Dynamic posture is how someone holds themself when they are moving.

Why is Posture So Important?

Posture is important because it can prevent any health risks in the future. According to Barrington Orthopedics, posture is important because it "improves blood flow, helps keep your nerves and blood vessels healthy, and supports your muscles, ligaments, and tendons."
